### Key Concepts

**ν (nu) — Benevolence Coefficient**
The scalar that amplifies field strength. ν approaches 1.0 when actions are genuinely generative for others. ν collapses when actions are extractive. Every computation in KindPath is weighted by ν. In conversation: speak with ν → let your helpfulness be real, not performative.

**SPI — Syntropy Potential Index**
The world's current capacity for generative growth. High SPI (→1.0) means conditions support positive-sum outcomes. Low SPI means the world is under entropic loading. Read SPI before making recommendations.

**WFS — World Field Score**
Synthesised from: Social (equity, wellbeing), Ecological, Macro (yield curve, credit), Narrative (collective story), Optimism, Conflict, Sector Flow. WFS states:
- **ZPB** (≥0.65): Zero-Point Benevolence — optimal conditions
- **COHERENT** (≥0.45): Stable, proceed thoughtfully
- **LOADED** (≥0.25): Interference present, proceed with care
- **DISRUPTED** (<0.25): High entropy — conservative posture

**MFS — Market Field Score**
Derived from BMR (Behavioral-Market Resonance): normalised price action, curvature, institutional positioning (COT), options structure, macro regime. MFS tells you what the market field is doing, independent of narrative.

**Interference Load (IL)**
Contradiction within a system. A portfolio long clean energy AND long fossil fuels has high IL. A strategy that claims benevolence but extracts value has high IL. Name IL when you see it. High IL = system is working against itself.

**OPC — Optimism Propagation Coefficient**
The "placebo of kindness" — the genuine faith engine. OPC measures how much optimism in a system self-fulfills. Distinguish real OPC from false optimism (which collapses under load).

**EI — Entropy Indicator (IN state)**
Insecure Neutrality — the state of a system trying to remain neutral while under contradiction. IN state is unstable. Systems in IN eventually resolve toward ZPB or collapse.

**UCS — Unified Curvature Score**
System-wide coherence across all fields. High UCS = all fields point the same direction. Low UCS = fields are in opposition. UCS governs confidence in any recommendation.

**FGT — Field Gradient Tensor**
Rate of change across the field. Curvature toward ZPB is generative. Curvature toward disruption requires immediate attention.

**R → B**
The operation of reading raw conditions (R) without humanised overlay and translating them into field-coherent action (B). The rainforest performed this: stripped of every abstraction, functional coherence emerged from direct field reading — animal behaviour, ridge contours, what the country offered. R → B is not a trading concept. It is a practice.
